Why did we make this?

Our team initially planned to create a FPS; a First-Person Social Network. However, as time progressed, we realized that making a social network in 3D is useless. There would be no reason to socialize in 3D since it would just be more of a hassle to get things done such as chatting or posting on a wall. It may look awesome, but it wouldn't be practical to use.

So instead, we decided to transform whatever we had for the social-network into a 3D maze game. This would allow players to compete against other players through FaceBook and race against each other to test their luck and memory.

We found that the real fun of the game is inviting a bunch of your friends and have everyone in cheat mode, flying around and chasing each other. The simple graphics and choppy movements of the player avatars just seem to be quite the hilarious scene.
